## Problem Details

### 1. Recyclable and Low Fat Products

* Retrieve products that are both recyclable and low fat.
* Concepts: `SELECT`, `WHERE`, Multiple Conditions.

### 2. Find Customer Referee

* Find customers who were not referred by a specific referee.
* Concepts: `WHERE`, `IS NULL`, Conditional Filtering.

### 3. Big Countries

* Identify countries with a large area or population.
* Concepts: Logical Operators (`OR`), Filtering.

### 4. Article Views I

* Find authors who viewed their own articles.
* Concepts: `DISTINCT`, Filtering Records.

### 5. Invalid Tweets

* Find tweets whose content exceeds the allowed character limit.
* Concepts: String Functions, `CHAR_LENGTH()` / `LENGTH()`.
